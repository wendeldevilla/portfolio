My Top 5 Players for Dota 2!

ABED

Abed "Abed" Yusop (born August 2, 2000) is a Filipino player who is currently playing for Shopify Rebellion.

Dyrachyo

Anton "dyrachyo" Shkredov (born September 5, 2001) is a Russian player who is currently playing for Gaimin Gladiators.

Iceiceice

Daryl "iceiceice" Koh (born June 17, 1990) is a Singaporean player who is currently playing for Bleed Esports.

Puppey

Clement "Puppey" Ivanov (born March 6, 1990) is an Estonian player who is currently playing for Team Secret.

Jerax

Jesse "JerAx" Vainikka (born May 7, 1992) is a Finnish Dota 2 player who last coached Team Liquid.
