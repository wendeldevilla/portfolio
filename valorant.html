My Top 5 Players for Valorant!

Derke

Nikita "Derke" Sirmitev (born February 6, 2003) is a Finnish-Russian player who currently plays for Fnatic. He is a former Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player known for his time in SkitLite and KOVA Esports.

ZmjKK

Zheng "ZmjjKK" Yongkang (born March 3, 2004) is a Chinese player who is currently playing for EDward Gaming.

Aspas

Erick "aspas" Santos (born June 15, 2003) is a Brazilian player who is currently playing for LOUD.

Jinggg

Wang "Jinggg" Jing Jie (born July 29, 2003) is an Singaporean player who currently plays for Paper Rex.

cNed

Mehmet "cNed" İpek (born January 18, 2002) is a Turkish player who is currently playing for Natus Vincere.
